    "# Customer Segmentation Analysis using RFM and K-Means Clustering\n",
    "\n",
    "## Project Overview\n",
    "The objective of this project is to segment an e-commerce company's customers into distinct groups based on their purchasing behaviour using **RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) Analysis** and the **K-Means Clustering Algorithm**.\n",
    "\n",
    "Customer segmentation enables businesses to understand different customer groups and develop targeted marketing strategies to improve customer retention, increase sales, and maximize customer lifetime value.\n",
    "\n",
    "## Objectives\n",
    "\n",
    "- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing.\n",
    "- Calculate customer purchasing behaviour using RFM Analysis.\n",
    "- Standardize customer features using StandardScaler.\n",
    "- Apply K-Means Clustering to segment customers.\n",
    "- Determine the optimal number of clusters using the Elbow Method.\n",
    "- Visualize customer segments.\n",
    "- Provide business insights and marketing recommendations.\n",
    "\n",
    "## Dataset\n",
    "\n",
    "**Dataset:** Online Retail Dataset\n",
    "\n",
    "The dataset contains transactional data from a UK-based online retail store.\n",
    "\n",
    "It includes:\n",
    "\n",
    "- Invoice Number\n",
    "- Product Description\n",
    "- Quantity Purchased\n",
    "- Invoice Date\n",
    "- Unit Price\n",
    "- Customer ID\n",
    "- Country\n",
    "\n",
    "## Tools\n",
    "\n",
    "- Python\n",
    "- Pandas\n",
    "- NumPy\n",
    "- Matplotlib\n",
    "- Seaborn\n",
    "- Scikit-learn\n",
    "- Jupyter Notebook\n",
    "\n",
    "## Project Workflow\n",
    "\n",
    "### 1. Data Inspection\n",
    "\n",
    "- Loaded the dataset\n",
    "- Checked data types\n",
    "- Examined missing values\n",
    "- Identified duplicate records\n",
    "\n",
    "### 2. Data Cleaning\n",
    "\n",
    "- Removed records with missing Customer IDs\n",
    "- Removed rows with missing product descriptions\n",
    "- Removed cancelled transactions\n",
    "- Removed transactions with negative quantity\n",
    "- Removed transactions with negative unit prices\n",
    "\n",
    "### 3. Feature Engineering\n",
    "\n",
    "Created a new feature:\n",
    "\n",
    "- **TotalAmount = Quantity × UnitPrice**\n",
    "\n",
    "### 4. RFM Analysis\n",
    "\n",
    "Calculated three customer behaviour metrics:\n",
    "\n",
    "- **Recency** – Number of days since the customer's last purchase\n",
    "- **Frequency** – Number of unique purchases made\n",
    "- **Monetary** – Total amount spent by each customer\n",
    "\n",
    "### 5. Data Standardization\n",
    "\n",
    "Applied **StandardScaler** to ensure all RFM features contributed equally during clustering.\n",
    "\n",
    "### 6. Customer Segmentation\n",
    "\n",
    "Applied **K-Means Clustering**.\n",
    "\n",
    "Used the **Elbow Method** to determine the optimal number of clusters.\n",
    "\n",
    "**Optimal Number of Clusters (K): 4**\n",
    "\n",
    "### 7. Data Visualization\n",
    "\n",
    "Created:\n",
    "\n",
    "- Elbow Method Plot\n",
    "- Number of Customers per Cluster\n",
    "- Frequency vs Monetary Scatter Plot\n",
    "- Recency vs Monetary Scatter Plot\n",
    "\n",
    "## Key Findings\n",
    "\n",
    "The analysis identified four customer segments:\n",
    "\n",
    "### Cluster 0 – Regular Customers\n",
    "\n",
    "- Moderate purchase frequency\n",
    "- Moderate spending\n",
    "- Opportunity for increased engagement\n",
    "\n",
    "### Cluster 1 – Inactive / At-Risk Customers\n",
    "\n",
    "- Long time since last purchase\n",
    "- Low spending\n",
    "- Require re-engagement campaigns\n",
    "\n",
    "### Cluster 2 – VIP Customers\n",
    "\n",
    "- Highest purchase frequency\n",
    "- Highest spending\n",
    "- Most valuable customer segment\n",
    "\n",
    "### Cluster 3 – Loyal Customers\n",
    "\n",
    "- Frequent purchases\n",
    "- High spending\n",
    "- Strong candidates for loyalty rewards\n",
    "\n",
    "## Business Recommendations\n",
    "\n",
    "- Reward VIP customers with exclusive offers and premium services.\n",
    "- Implement personalized marketing campaigns for loyal customers.\n",
    "- Launch re-engagement campaigns for inactive customers.\n",
    "- Encourage regular customers to purchase more frequently through loyalty programs and targeted promotions."
